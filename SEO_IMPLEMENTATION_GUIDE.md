## Common SEO Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't do this:**
- Keyword stuffing in content
- Duplicate content across pages
- Slow page loading
- Poor mobile experience
- Hidden text or cloaking
- Buying backlinks

✅ **Do this instead:**
- Natural keyword integration (1-2% density)
- Unique content per page
- Optimize images & code
- Mobile-first design
- Transparent, visible content
- Earn links naturally
